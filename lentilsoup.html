Lentil Soup

Description

A yellow lebanese dish very much enjoyed by me

Best eaten as an appetizer with crunchy bread and a squeeze of lemon.

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • Onions
  • lentils
  • carrots
  • cumin
  • coriander
  • salt&bread

Steps:

  1. Wash the lentils
  2. put butter into pan
  3. add lentils potatoes carrots tumeric and cumin and stir
  4. Add boiled water and let it simmer for about 45min
  5. lightly mix the soup together with a blender
  6. return the mix to the pot and add salt and pepper to taste
  7. Enjoy!
